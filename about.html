About me

Among the major courses that I studied are; Artificial Intelligence, Expert System, C++, Java, Database, and Data communication. I also actively participated in various curricular activities to further sharpen my personal skills and to develop my capability. I am certain that the undergone courses and co-curricular activities have equipped me with good analytical as well as interpersonal skills. I have been equipped, through my working experience, with the needed skills to deal with a multi-cultural working environments and required me to formulate planning strategies in order to achieve study and work objectives.
